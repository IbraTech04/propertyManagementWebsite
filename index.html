About Our CEO

Hi! My name is Ibrahim Chehab, and I own TechMaster Property Management Ltd. I grew up in Mississauga. I started off my career as a landlord and realized that a lot of property management companies were horrible. A lot of them ended up taking my money and not providing me any tenants! And a lot of the companies with a rental guarantee were way to expensive. I knew I wasn't the only person dealing with the phenomenon, so I figured I'd do something about it. That's when I founded TechMaster Property Management, an affordable property management company with a rental guarantee. Since then, my company has grown to be one of the biggest property management companies in the world, offering more services than anyone at the same price-point!
